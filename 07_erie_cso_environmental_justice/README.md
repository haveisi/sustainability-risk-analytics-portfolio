## Erie CSO Environmental Justice GIS Analysis

### Business Problem

Communities impacted by aging wastewater infrastructure often face overlapping exposure to flood risk, pollution burden, and social vulnerability, while organizations lack integrated spatial analytics for evaluating environmental justice and infrastructure resilience challenges.

### Solution

Developed GIS-based environmental justice and infrastructure-risk analysis workflows integrating combined sewer overflow (CSO) exposure, flood risk, demographic vulnerability, historical redlining patterns, and ArcPy automation.

### Tools

ArcGIS Pro | ArcPy | Python | Spatial Analysis | Environmental Justice Analytics

### Strategic Insights

* Identified concentrated overlap between CSO exposure and socially vulnerable communities
* Evaluated relationships between flood risk, historical disinvestment, and wastewater infrastructure burden
* Improved visibility into environmental justice and infrastructure resilience priorities
