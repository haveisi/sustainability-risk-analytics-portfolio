## Environmental Risk & Justice Mapping

### Business Problem

Communities facing environmental infrastructure challenges often experience overlapping exposure to climate vulnerability, pollution risk, flooding, and social inequities, while organizations lack integrated spatial analytics for evaluating environmental risk concentration and resilience gaps.

### Solution

Developed GIS-based environmental risk and vulnerability mapping workflows integrating climate exposure, infrastructure stress, flood risk, and environmental justice indicators to support sustainability and resilience analysis.

### Tools

ArcGIS Pro | GIS | Python | Spatial Analytics | Environmental Risk Assessment

### Strategic Insights

* Identified concentrated environmental and infrastructure vulnerability zones
* Evaluated overlap between climate exposure and social vulnerability indicators
* Improved visibility into environmental justice and resilience planning priorities
