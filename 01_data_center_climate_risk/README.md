## Data Center Climate & Infrastructure Risk Analytics

### Business Problem
Data centers face increasing exposure to climate, water, energy, and infrastructure-related risks, while organizations often lack integrated analytical systems to evaluate operational vulnerability, resilience challenges, and long-term sustainability risks associated with hyperscale infrastructure expansion.

### Solution
Developed GIS-based climate and infrastructure risk analytics workflows integrating flood exposure, water stress, infrastructure vulnerability, operational risk screening, and sustainability visualization to support resilience planning and strategic decision-making.

### Tools
ArcGIS Pro | Python | Power BI | GIS | Climate-Risk Analytics | Spatial Analysis

### Strategic Insights
- Identified overlapping exposure to flood-prone and infrastructure-vulnerable areas
- Evaluated operational risks linked to water stress and energy dependencies
- Improved visibility into infrastructure resilience and ESG-related operational risks
