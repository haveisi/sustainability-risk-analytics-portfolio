## Scope 3 & Regenerative Agriculture Analytics

### Business Problem
Agricultural supply chains represent major Scope 3 and FLAG emissions sources, while organizations often lack analytical systems for evaluating emissions hotspots, fertilizer sensitivity, regenerative agriculture scenarios, and sustainability performance across food and agricultural systems.

### Solution
Developed agricultural GHG accounting and regenerative agriculture analytics workflows integrating emissions estimation, fertilizer sensitivity analysis, scenario modeling, and sustainability dashboards to evaluate carbon hotspots and reduction opportunities.

### Tools
Python | Power BI | GIS | GHG Protocol | Agricultural Emissions Analytics

### Strategic Insights
- Identified fertilizer-intensive emissions hotspots across agricultural systems
- Evaluated regenerative agriculture reduction pathways and scenario impacts
- Improved visibility into Scope 3 and FLAG emissions exposure
