## ESG Disclosure & Sustainability Reporting Analytics

### Business Problem
Organizations face increasing investor, regulatory, and stakeholder pressure to improve ESG transparency and climate accountability, yet many sustainability disclosures contain inconsistent methodologies, incomplete Scope 3 coverage, weak comparability, and limited decision-useful metrics.

### Solution
Conducted ESG disclosure benchmarking and sustainability reporting analysis to evaluate emissions transparency, disclosure quality, reporting gaps, and climate-accountability performance across organizations.

### Tools
Python | ESG Analytics | GHG Protocol | Sustainability Reporting | Content Analysis

### Strategic Insights
- Identified major Scope 3 disclosure and reporting gaps
- Evaluated inconsistencies across sustainability reporting frameworks
- Improved visibility into ESG reporting maturity and climate-accountability readiness
