## Climate Action Plan Analytics & Evaluation

### Business Problem

Climate action plans often contain ambitious mitigation and resilience targets but vary significantly in implementation quality, accountability mechanisms, and operational readiness, limiting effective climate governance and long-term sustainability planning.

### Solution

Developed analytical workflows and evaluation frameworks for benchmarking climate action plans across mitigation, adaptation, governance, implementation readiness, emissions targets, and resilience integration dimensions.

### Tools

Python | Climate Policy Analytics | Sustainability Evaluation | Data Visualization

### Strategic Insights

* Identified implementation and accountability gaps across climate action plans
* Evaluated resilience integration and mitigation strategy alignment
* Improved comparative analysis of climate-policy effectiveness and implementation readiness
